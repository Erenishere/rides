Executive Mobility Solutions

Premium Chauffeur Service for Business, Events, and Executive Mobility

Business travel demands more than just transportation — it requires reliability, flexibility, and precision. FC Rides is built from the ground up for corporate clients who expect every journey to reflect their brand standards.

Whether you're moving executives to critical meetings, welcoming clients from the airport, or organizing transportation for an entire event, our technology-driven chauffeur service ensures your people and guests travel on time, in comfort, and without friction.

Why Corporate Clients Choose FC Rides

Intelligent Travel Infrastructure

We go beyond standard car service — our platform uses AI-assisted dispatch and predictive routing to guarantee on-time arrivals, even during peak hours. Every booking is optimized in real time for traffic, weather, and flight changes.

Private Client Accounts

All corporate partners receive a dedicated Private Client account that centralizes every part of your transportation operations.

  • Book rides for executives, guests, or clients under one dashboard
  • Add riders without sharing payment details
  • View trip history, invoices, and analytics in one place
  • Apply corporate discounts or partner commission structures

Flexible Billing & Payment Control

Our Payment Toggle gives you total control:

  • Rider Pays: Passenger pays directly at booking
  • Company Pays: Ride is billed to your corporate account and invoiced monthly

You can even set billing preferences per rider, per department, or per event.

Brand-Enhancing Experience

Every ride is designed to reflect the professionalism of your company. With Comfort Profile™, preferences like music, climate, and refreshments are remembered and applied automatically — ensuring that every executive feels valued before they even step into the car.
